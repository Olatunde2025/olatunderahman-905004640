About Football Agency Sierra Leone

Our mission, vision and story.

History

Founded in 2018, Football Agency Sierra Leone is dedicated to developing local talent and creating pathways for professional careers internationally.

Vision

To be the leading football management agency in West Africa—discovering, developing and promoting Sierra Leonean talent.

Mission

  • Identify talented players across Sierra Leone.
  • Provide training, trials and exposure to international scouts.
  • Negotiate fair contracts and manage careers professionally.
